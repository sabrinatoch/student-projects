About Us

GreaseMonkey University provides affordable courses for anybody looking to learn some car mechanic skills. We are a family owned and operated company located right beside Ottawa, founded by Konstantin Tochkov and his wife, Elissa in 1985. The Bulgarian couple started the auto shop when the movie "Back to the Future" came out, which inspired the signature Delorean design and slogan. We've helped many save hundreds of thousands on car repairs and maintenance tasks by teaching them to do it themselves! Our aim is to get you to be comfortable under your hood, and get you back to YOUR garage!

Our highly qualified staff is committed to offering the best learning experience for people at any level, whether beginner or expert. We offer flexible hours and affordable courses. Check out what some of our students have to say. If you have any questions, please contact us, we're more than happy to help answer them!
