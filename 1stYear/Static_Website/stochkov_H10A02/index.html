About Us

GreaseMonkey Univeristy provides affordable courses for anybody looking to learn some car mechanic skills. We are a family owned and operated company located right beside Ottawa, founded by Konstantin Tochkov and his wife, Elissa in 1985. The Bulgarian couple started the auto shop when the movie "Back to the Future" came out, which inspired the signature Delorean design and slogan. We've helped many save hundreds of thousands on car repairs and maintenance tasks by teaching them to do it themselves! Our aim is to get you to be comfortable under your hood, and get you back to YOUR garage!

Location: 17 Promenade Oval, Gatineau, QC, J9H 1T9
